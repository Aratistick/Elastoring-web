ELAST-O-RING

Located in Mumbai, India, a technological rubber product manufacturing unit named Elast-O-Ring An ISO 9001:2015, Was established in the year 2003. The main feature in Elast-O-Ring is to manufacture Moulded Rubber products, Extruded profiles, Gaskets, and Food Grade products for sealing purpose.
